About Us

Silvenia Writerhood is a writer organization founded by Kairus Silvenia in 2016, dedicated to nurturing and supporting aspiring writers. The organization provides a platform for budding authors to hone their writing skills, collaborate with open-minded individuals, and receive guidance from professional writers. Through workshops, writing contests, mentorship, and events, Silvenia Writerhood encourages creativity, storytelling, and literary growth. More than an organization, Silvenia is a communityâ€”a paradise for writers. Silvenia Writerhood believes in the power of words to connect worlds, bridging gaps between cultures, experiences, and perspectives. By bringing together diverse voices, the organization fosters a global community of writers who share their stories and explore the endless possibilities of imagination. Whether through fiction, poetry, or essays, members are empowered to craft narratives that transcend boundaries, uniting people through the universal language of storytelling.
